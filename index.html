Town of Salem Wiki

  • Description

What happens if you're a Serial Killer, and you claim to be a doctor! And now the Mayor is asking for a doctor's ability. In a matter of seconds, you're going to be Lynched! D:

Fear no more! This Town of Salem Wiki application is developed for you to quickly find all the information you need.

New? So what is Town of Salem?
"Town of Salem is a browser-based game that challenges players on their ability to convincingly lie as well as detect when other players are lying. The game ranges from 7 to 15 players. These players are randomly divided into alignments - Town, Mafia, Serial Killers, Arsonists and Neutrals. If you are a Town member (the good guys) you must track down the Mafia and other villains before they kill you. The catch? You don't know who is a Town member and who is a villain. If you are an evil role, such as a Serial Killer, you secretly murder town members in the veil of night and try to avoid getting caught."
- www.blankmediagames.com

Currently the application can:
1) View all Roles
- Includes 3 separate buttons "Town/Mafia/Neutral" for quick access
- Each role page includes the role alignment, abilities, goal(s), note(s), investigation results & win conditions!

SimpleDice

  • Description
  • Media
  • Changelog

Simple Dice is an android application aimed to give users a dice application rich in functionality, but yet simple to use. For instance, instead of putting fancy dice sides, Simple Dice gives you an option to use traditional numbers, making it clearer. Furthermore, Simple Dice is 100% Ad-Free, which means you wouldn't have any of those annoying little advertisement banners bugging you anywhere in the application.

Features:

  • 100% Ad-Free
  • Simple To Use
  • Roll Up-To 3 Dices
  • Beautiful Dice Animation

Simple Explosive Snow Ball

  • Description
  • Media
  • Changelog

As its name suggest, Simple Explosive Snow Ball, also known as ESB would make SnowBalls explode on impact. This simple plugin allows players to have fun with snowBalls, which would have been utterly useless. This plugin will be very helpful in faction servers, donator perks and much more!
